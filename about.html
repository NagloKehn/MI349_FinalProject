About Kehn Kore Games

Kehn Kore Games is an indie game studio dedicated to crafting innovative gaming experiences that blend nostalgic, retro-inspired aesthetics with modern game design. Our creative focus centers on delivering engaging gameplay that respects the foundations of classic gaming while pushing boundaries with experimental mechanics and thoughtful player experiences.

We draw inspiration from the golden age of gaming, where gameplay and creativity were paramount. Our portfolio showcases our commitment to diverse game design—from arcade-style action to strategic roguelike challenges—each title exploring unique mechanics designed to captivate and challenge players.

Our Vision

At Kehn Kore Games, we believe that exceptional game design transcends graphics and technology. We focus on crafting unique mechanics that create meaningful player experiences—whether through innovative control schemes, emergent gameplay systems, or unexpected narrative engagement. Every project is an opportunity to explore what makes games resonate with players.

Our portfolio reflects our philosophy: diverse genres unified by a commitment to quality, creativity, and player-centric design. We're passionate about pushing creative boundaries while honoring the timeless principles that make gaming memorable and immersive.
